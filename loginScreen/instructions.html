Four teams train doing mission in the training complex. Team numbers are displayed above the control tables.

Players can collaborate, writing code together or alternating who controls a remotely operated robot.

At a workstation, press the ESC key, and click on the monitor to choose missions to help people with the hurricane.

Each robot has a number of camera views. You can select the camera that is most useful for your mission.

The grid is helpful to find the distance in meters between locations. You can also get coordinates for flying drones.
